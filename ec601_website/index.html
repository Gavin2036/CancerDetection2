How it works?

A cancer tumor can have thousands of genetic mutations. Our machine learning algorithm distinguishes the mutations that contribute to tumor growth from the neutral mutations.

Cancer Detection

Upload your genetic mutation information. Our Machine Learning algorithm that, using this knowledge base as a baseline, automatically classifies genetic variations.

Research Database

Thousands of genetic mutation samples manually classified by the clinical pathologist based on evidence from text-based clinical literature.
